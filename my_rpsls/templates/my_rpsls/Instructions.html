Instructions

The game is an expansion on the game Rock, Paper, Scissors. Each player picks a variable and reveals it at the same time. The winner is the one who defeats the others. The game will be played for three rounds. Winning a round will earn a player 1 points, whereas loosing will result in a 1 point loss. A draw gives 0 points to each player. The winner of the game is the player who earned the most points in the three rounds. The rules are as follows:

Rock crushes Scissors and Rock crushes Lizard

Scissors cuts Paper and Scissors decapitates Lizard

Paper covers Rock and Paper disproves Spock

Lizard poisons Spock and Lizard eats Paper

Spock smashes Scissors and Spock vaporizes Rock
